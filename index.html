Flowers and Lillies

Grass and Brass with white accents,
this field is one with many color depths
A little blue butterfly flutters along with his friends
they stopped and stared as they seemed to lose all their sense

Roses, Lavender, Sage and Thyme
all of them together, you would think it was a crime
the little butterflies were in awe of this so they rested on some vine
all they could ponder on was the beautiful sopecimens and their shine

The wind blew, and rain drops were felt
so the little butterflies had to go as their hearts began to melt
this wasn't the end for they had more fields to see
they all fluttered away happily as they said bye to the smell of the lily

The End
